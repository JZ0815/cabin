The Ultimate Adventure Under the Smokies Awaits!

Step into an underground world where a consistent 58-degree climate welcomes you year-round. Have you ever pondered which state takes the crown for the most caves in the US? It's Tennessee, boasting an impressive tally of over 8,350 registered caves.

Among these, Forbidden Caverns in Sevierville stands distinctively as one of America's most awe-inspiring subterranean wonders. Dive into its depths and let your senses be dazzled by the glittering formations, imposing natural chimneys, mesmerizing grottos, and the tranquility of a crystal-clear stream.

But the marvel doesn't end with the visuals. The cavern tour offers an engaging and educational journey, enhanced by innovative lighting effects and spearheaded by our expert guides. Every step you take is safe and secure, with well-lit pathways and handrails in place.

Considering a base to explore these natural wonders? Cosby is an excellent choice! Living in or staying at Cosby places you in proximity to Forbidden Caverns, making it convenient to experience this marvel while soaking in the beauty of Cosby's scenic surroundings.

So, why hold back? Dive deep into the magic of the underground and let Forbidden Caverns become a cherished memory!
